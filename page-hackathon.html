Hackathon

The first 2-day hackathon in Cyprus
just got bigger and funner.

{% include ticket-btn.html %}

Friday Sept 6h – Saturday Sept 7th

The University of Cyprus

Free, but ticketed

Join 80+ creative developers, designers and ideators for 30 hours of hacking, designing, tinkering and general making of awesome things – web, mobile, hardware, anything goes. You can come with a team or find a team on the day. The winners will get great prizes and the chance to present their projects at Insights, in front of an eclectic and international audience.

Judging & how to win

You can design, sketch and conceptualise beforehand, but all code must be fresh. Maximum of 5 people per team. Teams have full ownership of whatever they build. Choose a good problem to solve. Have fun!

What the judges are looking for

Problem 20%

Solution 40%

Marketability 20%

Surprise Factor 20%
