The Startup Prize (Opt-in only)

Participation in CIIM's Accelerator Program

The Entrepreneurship and Innovation Centre (ENTICE) of CIIM is launching an acceleration program that will help transform bright ideas into start-up companies and ambitious people into capable entrepreneurs, arming them with knowledge, tools and networks in order to enhance the competitiveness of Cyprus, the region and beyond.

This prize is for teams that wish to bring their hackathon project to market. It will be judged separately from the rest of the prizes and will favour the marketability of the idea and the proposed solution.

The first 100 people to arrive on the day will each get $100 of free cloud hosting from the awesome folks at Amazon Web Services, so get there early!

Judging & how to win

You can design, sketch and conceptualise beforehand, but all code must be fresh. Your team must be a maximum of 5 people. You will have full ownership & IP of whatever you build. Choose a good problem to solve. Have fun!

What the judges are looking for

Problem 20%

Solution 40%

Marketability 20%

Surprise Factor 20%
